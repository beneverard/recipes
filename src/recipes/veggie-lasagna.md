### Veggie ragu

* Cook onions, carrots and celery for 8-10 minutes until soft
* Add garlic and oregano, cook for 30 seconds
* Add tomato paste and cook for 1 minute
* Add chopped tomatoes, sugar, balsamic vinegar, salt and pepper, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until thick

### Cheese sauce

* Melt butter in a saucepan
* Whist in flour and cook for 1 minute (do not brown)
* Slowly whisk in warm milk until smooth
* Simmer until thickened and silky
* Add cheddar, salt and pepper

### Assembly

* Alternate layers starting with ragu, ending with cheese sauce
* Top with extra cheese
* Cover with foil and cook at 180Â°C for 30 minutes
* Uncover and cook for 12-15 minutes until golden brown
* Rest for at least 15 minutes before slicing
