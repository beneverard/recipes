ingredients:
  - 250g day old rice
  - 1 small onion (diced)
  - 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  - 1 tsp curry powder
  - 1/2 tbp tumeric
  - 100g Quorn chicken pieces
  - 1 red bell pepper (diced)
  - 1 green bell pepper (diced)
  - 1 medium carrot (grated or julienned)
  - 50g frozen peas
  - 3 tbsp soy sauce
  - 1 tbsp water
  - Salt and pepper to taste
---
* Lightly fry Quorn chicken pieces and set aside.
* Meanwhile, saut√© the onions and garlic until translucent.
* Add the curry power and tuemric, stir for 30 seconds.
* Add the bell peppers, carrot, peas and Quorn chicken pieces, cook for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
* Add the cooked rice and combine well.
* Mix in the soy sauce, stir for 1-2 minutes until the rice is well heated.
* Add salt and pepper to taste.
